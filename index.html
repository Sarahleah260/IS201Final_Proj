About

Here is a brief professional History.

    High School Graduate

  • August 2021 March 2023

    Missionary Service

After a year, was promoted to a "Member Experience Officer" (meaning I am loan trained) at Mountain America. Currently rursuing a bachelor's degree at BYU, aspiring to Human Resources.
